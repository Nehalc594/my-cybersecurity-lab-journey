## What This Room Covers
- Purpose of network scanning and enumeration
- Introduction to Nmap as a reconnaissance tool
- Understanding how services are discovered on networks

## Key Concepts
- Enumeration is the foundation of most attacks and investigations
- Open ports reveal exposed services
- Scan results must be interpreted in context

## Security Perspective
- Offensive: attackers scan networks to identify entry points
- Defensive: defenders monitor and restrict scanning activity

## Key Takeaways
- Enumeration quality determines downstream success
- Every exposed service increases attack surface
- Network scanning is both a defensive and offensive activity
