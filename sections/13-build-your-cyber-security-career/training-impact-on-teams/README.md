# Room: Training Impact on Teams

**Section:** Build Your Cyber Security Career  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Status:** Completed  

---

## What This Room Covers
- Importance of training in cybersecurity teams
- How training improves security posture
- Relationship between skills and incident readiness

## Key Concepts
- Well-trained teams respond faster and more effectively
- Training reduces human error and misconfigurations
- Continuous learning keeps teams aligned with evolving threats

## Security Perspective
- Offensive: attackers exploit skill gaps and outdated knowledge
- Defensive: trained teams detect, respond, and recover more efficiently

## Key Takeaways
- Training is a strategic investment, not a one-time activity
- Team capability directly affects security outcomes
- Continuous improvement strengthens organizational resilience
